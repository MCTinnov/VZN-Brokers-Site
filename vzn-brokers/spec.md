# VZN Brokers Landing Page - Design Specification

## Project Overview

**Brand:** VZN Brokers
**Industry:** Business Funding / Financial Services
**Tagline:** "Business Funding Solutions Structured Fast!"
**Target Audience:** Small business owners seeking capital for growth, operations, or expansion

## Brand Identity

### Color Palette
- **Primary:** Charcoal Navy (#1a1f2e) - Dark, professional foundation
- **Secondary:** Metallic Gold (#c9a961) - Premium, trustworthy accent
- **Background:** Off-white (#f8f6f3) - Clean, trustworthy canvas
- **Text:** Dark slate (#2d3748) - Readable, professional
- **Light accent:** Champagne (#f5e6c8) - Subtle warmth

### Typography
- **Headlines:** Playfair Display (serif) - Elegant, established trust
- **Body:** Inter (sans-serif) - Clean, modern, readable
- **Accent:** Montserrat - Bold call-to-action elements

## Visual Direction
- Clean, minimal, professional aesthetic
- Emphasis on trust and reliability
- Modern without being cold
- Premium without being pretentious

## Layout & Structure

### 1. Navigation
- Fixed top navigation
- Logo left, CTA button right
- Subtle backdrop blur on scroll

### 2. Hero Section (Video-Led)
- Full-viewport video background (atmospheric finance imagery)
- Centered headline with tagline
- Single prominent CTA button
- Muted overlay for text readability

### 3. Trust Indicators Bar
- Horizontal scroll of trust badges/stats
- "Trusted by 500+ Businesses"
- "Funded $50M+ in 2024"
- Quick stats in gold accent boxes

### 4. Services Section
- Clean card grid (2x3 on desktop)
- Icon + title + brief description
- Loan products offered
- Subtle hover animations

### 5. Why Choose Us Section
- Left: Key benefits list with checkmarks
- Right: Trust statistics
- Clean divider styling

### 6. Industries We Serve
- Visual grid of industry icons
- Clean, organized presentation
- Subtle category grouping

### 7. CTA Section
- Bold, centered call-to-action
- "Get Funded Today" messaging
- Contact form or phone number
- Gold accent on CTA button

### 8. Footer
- Contact information
- Quick links
- Disclaimer text
- Social links

## Motion & Interactions

- Subtle fade-in on scroll for sections
- Smooth hover states on buttons (scale + shadow)
- Card hover: subtle lift effect
- Navigation: backdrop blur transition
- No jarring or excessive animations

## Technical Requirements

- Single HTML file with embedded CSS
- Responsive (mobile-first)
- No external dependencies except Google Fonts
- Video: mp4 format, muted autoplay loop
- Images: optimized webp/jpg
